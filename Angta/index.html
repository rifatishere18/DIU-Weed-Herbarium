Angta

Local name: আঙটা ঘাস, কুশা ঘাস

English name: Torpedo Grass

Scientific name: Panicum repens

Family: Poaceae

Habit

Perennial grass.

Habitat

Found in the fields of Mustard, wheat, pulses, aus rice, jute and transplant aman rice. Also found in the levee of crop fields and irrigation channel, fallow lands and bank of ponds.

Growing season

Rabi and Kharif.

Associated crops

Rice, sugarcane, and other wetland crops.

Propagating organ

Rhizomes and stolons.

Leaf

Linear, narrow, with a rough texture.

Stem

Erect or creeping, slender, and hollow.

Inflorescence

Loose panicle with spikelets.

Root

Fibrous and spreading extensively through rhizomes.

Control measures

Regular mowing, herbicide application (e.g., glyphosate), and proper field drainage.
