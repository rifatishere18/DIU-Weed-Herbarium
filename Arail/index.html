Arail

Local name: সাউদার্ন কাটগ্রাস , ক্লাবহেড কাটগ্রাস এবং সোয়াম্প রাইস গ্রাস

English name: Swamp rice grass, Rice cut-grass, Lambedora grass

Scientific name: Leersia hexandra

Family: Poaceae

Habit

Perennial, creeping grass.

Habitat

Prefer moist condition. Found in the aus rice, jute and other low land crops.

Growing season

Rabi and Kharif (January to December).

Associated crops

Found commonly in rice paddies and wetland crops.

Propagating organ

Propagates through stolons and rhizomes.

Leaf

Blades narrowly linear, flat, rolled when dry, ligule membranous, the margins extending into sheath margins.

Stem

Decumbent at the base, rooting at the lower nodes, nodes swollen and curved with short hairs.

Inflorescence

A panicle, spikelets 1-flowered, oblong, strongly compressed laterally, hispidulous, sometimes glabrous.

Root

Fibrous root system adapted to waterlogged conditions.

Control measures

Managed using cultural practices, mechanical removal, and selective herbicides.
